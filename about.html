Hello there, I'm Yiwei.
A UX Designer/Product Designer
I graduated from Information Technology and Administrative management department (ITAM) at Central Washington University in summer 2020. Usable, valuable and aesthetic interaction is what drives me to learn UX. My journey started with information technology management, but I found the joy of creation. Currently, I am working in a edTech company as the CTO and UX designer in Kirkland, WA.
As a human who has a passion for human centered interaction design,I believe our society could be improved by more usable, useful, and sustainable products and services.
Since being a member of Interaction Design Foundation, I am focusing on the vision of design and technology. I dream of being the UX design rockstar that could design the product and service which users can be proud to use.
