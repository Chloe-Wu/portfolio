Mercer Island Telecom

Overview

Develop an official business website for Mercer Island Telecom and attract more people to discover the website and be interested in G-Suite Essential.

Role

Business Strategy, User Research, Wireframe, WordPress Developer, SEO Specialist

Method & Tool

Method: Competitive Research, Data analyze, Html5/Css, Optimize SEO (Keywords&backlinks)

Design Process




Define Problems

Mercer Island Telecom, a B2B digital transformation strategy consulting firm, currently needs a website as a platform for information presentation. In addition, the company is conducting a number of email marketing campaigns to sell G-Suite Essential products to help improve the way it works during the outbreak (WFH).




Problems


  • No website
  • Hard to find clients (unclear target clients)
  • unclear business mode
  • Unclear content strategy
  • Need more organic traffic

Result

Mercer Island Telecom needs not only the website, but also the clear and reliable business strategy. First, it needs to ensure the target clients on each email compaign with the specific solution, and simply the user flow. My responsibility is to design the wireframe, and developing it on WordPress. Besides,in order to attract more visitors, I work with marketing department on the email compaign strategy and the SEO optimization.




Competitive research

competitors

Cloudbakers-a cloud solutions and change management partner，it optimizes clients business with the latest cloud technology—it makes implementing clients’ cloud setup an enjoyable process by providing ongoing support, guidance and plenty of personality at every stage of clients’ transformation.

Cloudreach-the world’s leading independent multi-cloud services company. The company’s mission is to deliver the promise of cloud and drive extraordinary value for their customers. It helps enterprises win competitive advantage through successful cloud transformation. It is the go-to strategic partner for Amazon Web Services, Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure.br>

Common feature







While comparing these two companies, I notice that both of them have more than one solutions(softwares) for clients to choose. Unlike them, Mercer Island Telecom only focus on Google Suite, especially G Suite essentials to help companies solve the online collaboration working solution. Besides, their business mode is a bit different. Although both of them offer customize solution service, Cloudbaker created many basic solutions in advance which clients can preview and select. Cloudreach still prefer clients contact them and provide the company's information before getting any advice. After the discussion, the manager decided to follow Cloudreach's business mode.





Strategy

Cloudreach is a big company which already have stable volume and authority, but Mercer Island Telecom is not on that stage. Thus I need to figure our Mercer Island Telecom's SWOT. After the long time research, I did the SWOT analysis and presented to my manager.

What are our goals?- Sell G Suite Essentials to target group.

Who are our users? - (B2B) local company who is interested in digital transformation

What are their needs? - Digital transformation/ remote work

What is our value proposition?-Since Google Cloud is the only solution Mercer Island Telecom prefers, we would provide provide the product information in details on website and customize our service.





Mindmap

Development

Tools




Theme

The website domain was bought by Mr.Carlos Obando,the company CEO. After setting up the domain, we finally decide to use WordPress as our CMS. Carlos prefer to have the easy-editting website. And I recommended him to use the Divi theme. Its css/html5 editting module is easy for me to use, and its visual editting function would be friendly to my non technical collegues.







SEO

The SEO strategy I chose are: keyword research and backlink research. For example, I created keyword lists including general producti and service idea and competitors keywords. And rank them by volume and keyword difficulty. The backlink research would be focusing on both "follow" and "non follow" which can be checked from source code. Since I added the Google Analytic tracking code into the website 3 month ago, traffic has been increased 346.3%.




Update Info

The website development is not finished since Google updated its G Suite Essential product to be Google Workspace last month. To avoid unnecessary risk, the company's next step will focus on writing the new content.
